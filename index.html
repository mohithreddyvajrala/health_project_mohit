How It Works

Three simple steps from input to clinical risk output

1

Enter or Upload Data

Fill in the patient health form directly, or upload a scanned paper form for simulated processing.

2

Rule-Based Processing

The system applies clinical decision rules instantly — no server required, all processed in the browser.

3

Risk Level Output

A clear risk classification (Critical / High / Moderate / Low) is displayed with the triggering reason.

System Capabilities

📋

Direct Form Input

Structured form covering Temperature, Pain Level, Breathing Condition, Blood Pressure, and Emergency Indicators.

📷

Paper Scan Mode

Upload an image of a filled paper form. The system simulates OCR processing and generates a sample risk output.

⚙

Instant Rule Engine

Decision rules fire the moment you submit. No delay, no server roundtrip — results appear immediately.

📈

4-Level Risk Scale

Outputs one of four levels — Critical, High, Moderate, or Low — with a message and the triggering conditions.

Risk Classification Rules

How the rule engine maps inputs to risk levels

Risk Level Trigger Conditions Recommended Action
Critical Severe Pain OR Severe Breathing Difficulty OR Emergency Indicator selected Immediate medical attention required
High High Fever OR High Blood Pressure OR Moderate Pain Medical evaluation recommended
Moderate Mild Fever OR Slight Breathing Difficulty Monitor condition closely
Low All readings within normal range Stable condition — routine follow-up
